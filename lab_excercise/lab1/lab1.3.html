Customizing the Bar Chart

To customize this bar chart, you can make the following adjustments in the SVG code:

  • Change bar values by modifying the height and y attributes of the <rect> elements.
  • Alter bar colors by changing the fill attribute of the <rect> elements.
  • Resize the entire chart by adjusting the width and height attributes of the main <svg> element.
