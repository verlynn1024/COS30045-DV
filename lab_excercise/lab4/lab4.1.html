Design Studio Activity

Question 1:

How does the distribution of road fatalities vary across different road types and speed limits?

Answer:

  • X-axis (Category): Road Type (Column "National Road Type")
  • Y-axis (Values): Count of fatalities (Count of "Crash ID")
  • Legend: Speed Limit (Column "Speed Limit")

Question 2:

What is the relationship between time of day, day of the week, and the number of road fatalities?

Answer:

  • X-axis (Category): Day of week (Column "Day of week")
  • Y-axis (Values): Count of fatalities (Count of "Crash ID")
  • Legend: Time of day (Column "Time of day")

Question 3:

How do road fatalities differ across various age groups and remoteness areas in Australia?

Answer:

  • X-axis (Category): Age Group (Column "Age Group")
  • Y-axis (Values): Count of fatalities (Count of "Crash ID")
  • Legend: National Remoteness Areas (Column "National Remoteness Areas")

Question 4:

Make a sketch of how you think your visualisation might look and add it to this document.

Answer:

A dashboard containing:

  1. Line chart: Fatalities over time
  2. Bar chart: Fatalities by state
  3. Pie chart: Fatalities by road user type

Note: The actual visualization would be implemented using D3.js or a similar library.
